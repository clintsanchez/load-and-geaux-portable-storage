## What You Need to Know

### 1. Robot Rules Are Blocking AI Search
**What it means:** Your website told ChatGPT, Claude AI, and AI search engines not to index you.

**Why it matters:** 
- Missing from AI search results (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity AI)
- Competitors may appear in AI search; you don't
- Reduces visibility in emerging AI-powered search

**Our recommendation:**
- Allow Google and mainstream AI crawlers
- Block specific competitors if desired
- *Decision needed from you: Do you want to appear in AI search, or prefer privacy?*

### 2. Google Business Profile Optimization
**What it means:** Local search optimization on Google Maps/Search.

**Why it matters:**
- 70% of customers search "storage Baton Rouge" on Google Maps
- Optimized profile = more calls, quotes, website traffic
- Photos, reviews, hours all impact visibility

**Action:** Complete Google Business Profile (your listing on Google Maps)

### 3. Search Engine Visibility
**What it means:** Making sure Google can find and rank your pages.

**Why it matters:**
- Better ranking = more organic traffic
- Currently missing local business schema (tells Google you're a local business)
- Missing full Local SEO setup

**Action:** Add LocalBusiness schema and complete GSC setup (1 hour work)

### 4. Analytics & Conversion Tracking
**What it means:** Measuring website performance and lead generation.

**Why it matters:**
- No way to know which marketing efforts work
- Can't optimize without data
- Missing baseline metrics

**Action:** Set up Google Analytics 4 + conversion tracking (2 hour work)
